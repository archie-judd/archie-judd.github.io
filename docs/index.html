Kieffer / Van Gogh

At the Royal Academy

The exhibition is at the back of the RA, in 3 rooms. Each room has one or two enormous Kieffer canvasses, and a handful of his smaller works. There are a few Van Goghs, but they feel like they're there to provide context rather than take the spotlight. The contrast between the two is stark.
